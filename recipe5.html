    Ramen Noodles

    🥗 Non-Vegetarian | Snacks/Main Course

    A warm, comforting bowl of rich broth, tender chicken, and flavorful noodles

    🍽 Servings

    2-3 People

    ⏱ Preparation Time

    30 Minutes

    🔥 Difficulty

    Medium

    🥗 Category

    Non-Vegetarian

    Chicken Ramen is a classic Japanese noodle soup known for its rich umami broth, tender chicken, and perfectly cooked noodles. This dish combines warmth, nutrition, and flavor in one bowl, making it ideal for both comfort meals and quick dinners.

    Ingredients

    For Broth

    • 2 cups chicken broth
    • 1 cup water
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tsp grated ginger
    • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
    • 1 tsp sesame oil

    For Ramen

    • 150g ramen noodles
    • 1 cup cooked shredded chicken
    • 1 boiled egg (halved)
    • ½ cup mushrooms (sliced)
    • ½ cup spinach / bok choy
    • 2 green onions (chopped)
    • Salt to taste
    • Chili flakes (optional)

    Step-by-Step Instructions

    1. Sauté garlic and ginger in sesame oil until fragrant.
    2. Add chicken broth, water, and soy sauce. Bring to boil.
    3. Add mushrooms and cook for 2–3 minutes.
    4. Cook noodles separately according to instructions.
    5. Add shredded chicken to broth and simmer.
    6. Add spinach or bok choy and cook briefly.
    7. Place noodles in serving bowl.
    8. Pour hot broth over noodles.
    9. Top with boiled egg and green onions.
    10. Serve hot and enjoy.

    Chef Tips

    • Use fresh broth for deeper flavor.
    • Keep noodles slightly firm (al dente).
    • Add toppings like corn, seaweed, or bamboo shoots.
    • Balance soy sauce carefully to avoid excess saltiness.
    • Soft-boiled eggs enhance authenticity.

    Storage Instructions

    Store broth and noodles separately in airtight containers in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. Reheat broth on stovetop and add freshly cooked noodles before serving.
