    Butter Chicken Masala

    🥗 Non-Vegetarian | Main Course

    A rich, creamy and iconic North Indian chicken curry

    🍽 Servings

    4 People

    ⏱ Preparation Time

    45 Minutes

    🔥 Difficulty

    Medium

    🥗 Category

    Non-Vegetarian

    Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani) Butter Chicken is a classic Indian dish made with marinated & grilled chicken (Tandoori chicken), simmered in a creamy tomato gravy/curry. The sauce is super silky, buttery, aromatic and mildly spicy. This lip-smacking and delicious dish is hugely popular among the Indian food lovers across the world.
    Though this dish has a lot of similarities with Chicken Tikka Masala, both smell and taste very different. Originally, Butter Chicken is a mild dish, does not include onions and is cooked with butter. But tikka masala is spicy, hot, includes onions and is cooked in oil.

    Ingredients

    Curry Base

    • 4 tbsp oil
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 4 tomatoes (chopped)
    • 2 onions (sliced)
    • 10 cloves garlic
    • 2 inch ginger
    • 2 tbsp cashew nuts
    • Whole spices (cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, bay leaf)
    • 2 cups water

    For Curry

    • 500g chicken (boneless pieces)
    • 2 cups fresh cream
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 tsp red chili powder
    • 1 tsp turmeric powder
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • 1 tsp coriander powder
    • 1 tsp cumin powder
    • 2 tsp chicken masala
    • Salt to taste
    • Kasuri methi

    Step-by-Step Instructions

    1. Heat oil and butter, sauté whole spices.
    2. Add onions, garlic, and ginger; cook until soft.
    3. Add tomatoes and cashews; cook briefly.
    4. Add water and boil for 5–10 minutes.
    5. Cool and grind into smooth paste.
    6. Heat butter and oil again, add powdered spices.
    7. Add prepared paste and cook well.
    8. Add salt, cream, and mix properly.
    9. Add chicken pieces and simmer until cooked.
    10. Add kasuri methi and garnish with coriander.

    Chef Tips

    • Use fresh and tender chicken for best taste.
    • Balance spices carefully for authentic flavor.
    • Do not overcook chicken to keep it juicy.
    • Use cream for smooth and rich texture.
    • Adjust gravy consistency as needed.

    Storage Instructions

    Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2–3 days. Reheat with a little water to maintain consistency.
