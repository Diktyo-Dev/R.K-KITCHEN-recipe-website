    Butter Paneer Masala

    🥗 Vegetarian | Main Course

    A rich, creamy North Indian curry loved by everyone

    🍽 Servings

    4 People

    ⏱ Preparation Time

    40 Minutes

    🔥 Difficulty

    Medium

    🥗 Category

    Vegetarian

    Paneer Butter Masala is one of the most popular North Indian curries known for its rich, creamy texture and mildly sweet-spicy taste. Made with soft paneer cubes in a buttery tomato gravy, it pairs perfectly with roti, naan, or rice.

    Ingredients

    Curry Base

    • 4 tbsp oil
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 4 tomatoes (chopped)
    • 2 onions (sliced)
    • 10 cloves garlic
    • 2 inch ginger
    • 2 tbsp cashew nuts
    • Whole spices (cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, bay leaf)
    • 2 cups water

    For Curry

    • 300g paneer cubes
    • 2 cups fresh cream
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 tsp red chili powder
    • 1 tsp turmeric powder
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • 1 tsp coriander powder
    • 1 tsp cumin powder
    • Salt to taste
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • Kasuri methi

    Step-by-Step Instructions

    1. Heat oil and butter, then sauté whole spices.
    2. Add onions, garlic, and ginger. Cook until soft.
    3. Add tomatoes and cashews, cook briefly.
    4. Add water and boil for 5–10 minutes.
    5. Cool and grind into smooth paste.
    6. Heat butter and oil again, add powdered spices.
    7. Add prepared paste and cook well.
    8. Add salt, sugar, and cream.
    9. Add paneer cubes and simmer.
    10. Add kasuri methi and garnish with coriander.

    Chef Tips

    • Use fresh paneer for best texture.
    • Balance sweetness and spice carefully.
    • Use cream for smooth consistency.
    • Do not overcook paneer to avoid hardness.
    • Adjust gravy thickness as needed.

    Storage Instructions

    Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for 2–3 days. Reheat on stovetop or microwave with a little water for consistency.
