    Chocolate Cake

    🥗 Vegetarian / Non-Vegetarian

    A rich, moist, and soft chocolate cake perfect for every occasion

    🍽 Servings

    20-24 People

    ⏱ Preparation Time

    45 Minutes

    🔥 Difficulty

    Medium

    🥗 Category

    Vegetarian

    Chocolate Cake is a classic dessert known for its rich flavor and soft, moist texture. This recipe is simple, made in one bowl, and delivers a deeply chocolatey taste that melts in your mouth. It is perfect for celebrations, birthdays, or as a delightful treat anytime.

    Ingredients

    Dry Ingredients

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 cups sugar
    • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
    • 2 tsp baking powder
    • 1.5 tsp baking soda
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp espresso powder (optional)

    Wet Ingredients

    • 1 cup milk
    • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
    • 2 eggs
    • 2 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1 cup boiling water

    Step-by-Step Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C) and prepare cake pans.
    2. In a large bowl, mix all dry ingredients.
    3. Add milk, oil, eggs, and vanilla. Mix well.
    4. Slowly add boiling water and mix until smooth.
    5. Pour batter into prepared pans.
    6. Bake for 30–35 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
    7. Cool the cake for 10 minutes, then remove from pans.
    8. Let it cool completely before frosting.

    Chef Tips

    • The batter will be thin — this makes the cake moist.
    • Use good quality cocoa powder for better flavor.
    • Do not overbake to avoid dryness.
    • Add coffee instead of water to enhance chocolate flavor.
    • Let cake cool completely before frosting.

    Storage Instructions

    Store the cake in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days or refrigerate for up to 5 days. You can also freeze the cake layers for up to 2 months.
